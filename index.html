About
Hello, I am Divyanshu, a final year BCA student at Dr. C.V. Raman University, Kota, Bilaspur. I am an aspiring developer and have been spending a considerable amount of my time in learning technologies and building small projects to enhance my development skills over the past few months.

My current techstack comprises of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, TailwindCSS and Tailblocks. With this techstack and an obsession to make interfaces more appealing I can built simple, sweet and smooth websites.

My projectlist includes 5 small projects (Digital Clock, Stopwatch, BMI Calculator, Age Calculator and ChatGPT's UI Clone) and 2 games (Stone Paper Scissor game and Guess The Number game). All these 6 projects have been deployed using github pages and are full responsive. Live links and Source Code links have been mentioned below in the Projects Section.
